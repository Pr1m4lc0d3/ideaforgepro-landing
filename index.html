The seven gates

Every idea must pass all seven. No shortcuts.

01

02

03

The outcome they're buying, at a price where they say yes without hesitation.

Something tangible that proves the value before the product exists — and does the selling for you.

Pre-build test

06

One channel, gone deep, aimed at people showing intent — not a scraped list.

07

Builder spec

Validated demand translated into the minimum build required to deliver the promise. Hand it off only now.
